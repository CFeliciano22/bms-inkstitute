About Us

Meet The Owner

Greetings, I am Brooke – a licensed esthetician and skilled PMU artist. My profound affection for the beauty realm has guided my journey for years, igniting an unwavering passion within me. My initial foray involved enhancing the radiance of friends through makeup, an endeavor that encompassed events like proms and weddings. Upon graduating high school, I ventured into the art of lash extensions, an endeavor that saw my clientele flourish.

Embarking on the path of higher education, I enrolled in esthetics school, diligently working to obtain licenses that would grant me deeper insights into the industry. A pivotal moment arrived when I was introduced to the world of PMU, a revelation that resonated deeply with my soul. This newfound passion is a testament to my commitment to engendering beauty and confidence in others.

My focus has now converged on refining eyebrow aesthetics, specializing in a range of permanent makeup services such as ombré powder brows, precise brow shaping, and transformative brow laminations. My aspiration is to not only captivate but empower individuals to embrace their beauty with self-assuredness.

Frequently Asked Questions and Disclaimers

- Ombré Powdered Brows soft shading is the newest permanent makeup technique using a digital machine. It is 100% customizable and is great for anyone who wants to enhance their eyebrows with a soft, and natural makeup-like finish. This method creates pixelated dots to mimic powder filled brows with no harsh front or outline. This look creates a soft, misty, powder filled brow similar to the look of makeup

- Ombré powder brows are great for those who want to redefine their eyebrows. Whether you have naturally thin brows, or your brow-ends are fading with age. We are able to reshape your brows by adding a larger or smaller angle to your arch or more detail to your ends and this is completely customized to your own preferences. This procedure is great for anyone who is looking for perfect eyebrows

- We advise you to wait 3 weeks prior/post treatment. You should wait until you have reached your desired outcome of your brows, before you do anything that will jeopardize your eyebrows. We also recommend avoiding heavy creams and serums 3 weeks prior/ post treatment in the eyebrow region.

- First, a topical anesthetic cream will be applied Consultation is done the day of the appointment. Once the consultation is over, we will take our time on shaping the perfect brows and selecting a pigment customized just for you. The tattoo treatment itself will take about 2-3 hour total for both eyebrows. Please keep in mind, that being indecisive will take away time from your session. Please be prepared by providing photos of your ideal brow shape at the appointment.

- Yes, you will customize the shape and color. Our artists will work with you and ask what your preferences are. We help guide you by giving you our expert opinions, but the decision will ultimately be yours.

- A topical anaesthetic will be applied before and during the procedure to minimize any discomfort. The pain level is minimal to none for most clients.

- Throughout the years after the procedure, your skin will naturally exfoliate, and your body will naturally metabolize the pigment. All methods generally will last 1-3 years depending on skin type, conditions, and how natural or intense you want the eyebrows to look. The Ombre shading method will generally last longer due to it covering more surface area in the treated region. It is important to use spf daily because heavy sun exposure will expedite the fading process

- Any new client with previous work done by another artist must receive approval before booking an appointment. Please contact me by text (305)-796-3430 or email Bms.inkstitute@gmail.com for eligibility

- During the healing time, it is crucial to avoid getting the eyebrows wet even by sweat. Heavy sun exposure could also impact the healing process of your brows. Heavy sun exposure will cause early signs of scarring during this fragile state of your skin. Please keep your makeup only on the perimeter of the eyebrows during this time and avoid penciling in your eyebrows until they are completely peeled & healed. Specific aftercare ointment and instructions will be sent home with you.

- We highly recommend booking an appointment at least 2 weeks prior to a special event. The average healing time is about 7-14 days but for few, may take longer. No down time is needed. You can certainly go to work the day after. Color boosts and touch ups are recommended for optimal results and are booked as needed.

- An allergic reaction to pigments is extremely rare. To avoid a potential reaction, we do offer a patch test. The patch test must be booked by a separate appointment (30min). During that time, we will test how your skin reacts to the numbing cream as well as how your body reacts to the pigment that is used. The pigment will be placed behind your ear and be left alone for assessment during the following 24-48 hrs.
